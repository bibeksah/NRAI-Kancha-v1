AI Chatbot - Embed Example

How to Embed

To embed this chatbot in your website, use the following code:

<iframe 
    src="https://your-domain.com/embed" 
    width="100%" 
    height="600px"
    style="border: none; border-radius: 12px;"
    title="AI Chatbot"
    allow="microphone"
></iframe>

  • ✅ English and Nepali language support
  • ✅ Text-to-speech responses
